5  Responsibilities of Institution

The provision of services and infrastructure shall be set up according to the circumstances. In the event that the infrastructure or services are still being set up, it is possible to refer to this in the FD policy and to use the review process to adapt to current circumstances. In terms of services, a distinction can be made between departments/institutions (e.g. library, computer centre, research department, etc.).

5.1 Basic

5.1.1 Which basic equipment/services are available, which are being expanded?

5.1.2 Is a competence centre available/should be established?

The [name of the institution] undertakes to create the conditions for compliance with the principles.

5.1.3 The university implements and maintains a basic research data infrastructure and thus ensures appropriate storage and technical availability of digital research data. Specific requirements must be coordinated and, if necessary, additionally financed.

5.2 Infrastructure

5.2.1 Which technical infrastructure for the FDM is available or is being set up?

5.2.2 Is there a repository?

An institutional and long-term archivable research data repository for storage and publication, for reference and re-use in accordance with the access rights defined by the scientists exists/is being set up.

5.3 Consulting/Education/Training

5.3.1 Is counseling and/or education and training offered?

5.3.2 Is there legal and/or patent advice?

5.3.3 Is support offered for creating a DMP?

The [name of the institution] advises the RDM from planning, through implementation to the end of the project, and offers appropriate education and training. This includes technical, organizational and legal advice on all areas of RDM and the support of its researchers, in particular in the preparation of data management plans as well as the selection and implementation of the appropriate archiving and, if necessary, publication strategy.

  • In the event that results worthy of protection arise, patent law advice is provided.

  • With its central facilities, [name of the institution] supports researchers in the plan- ning, collection and storage of research data, in the formulation and maintenance of standards for the handling of research data as well as through appropriate training and advisory services, which are developed in cooperation with the specialist disciplines.

5.4 Cooperation

5.4.1 Does the institution cooperate with other institutions?

The [name of the institution] cooperates with other scientific institutions and partici- pates in common standards and structures.
