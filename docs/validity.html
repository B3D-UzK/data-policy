6  Validity

6.1 Validity/Review

The name of the body that adopts the FD policy is required, as well as Date and place of adoption. A review process is important in order to adapt the FD policy to current circumstances and new developments. If possible, it is already determined at this point who is responsible for the review process. Monitoring can also be carried out as part of the review process. The results of the monitoring can be incorporated into the review.

6.1.1 By whom/which body was the FD policy adopted on what date and place?

  • The present policy was adopted on [date] by [person/panel] the [name of institution] in [place].

6.1.2 When does the FD policy come into force?

The policy takes effect on [date].

6.1.3 How long is the FD policy valid?

6.1.4 Will a review be carried out? At what intervals and by whom?

  • This policy is subject to a review process by [person/department/panel] every [num- ber] of years.

  • This policy shall be reviewed regularly, at least every [number] years, by the [per- son/department/panel] of the [name of institution] to ensure that it is up-to-date;

6.1.5 Is monitoring carried out? Is it possible to provide feedback on the FD

policy?

  • As part of the review, a monitoring will be carried out to verify compliance with legal and regulatory standards and to adapt the FD policy to infrastructural developments and current needs of researchers.

  • Feedback on the FD policy can be provided at [contact, e-mail address].

6.1.6 Should versioning be listed? What changes have been made?

  • This policy appears in version number [number] with the following changes.

6.2 Contact

Contact persons for the FD policy as well as for questions about FDM can be mentioned.

6.2.1 Who is the contact person for questions about RDM?

6.2.2 Who is the contact person for the FD policy?

  • The contact person for questions regarding FD policy is [contact person/department].

  • If you have any questions about FDM, please contact [contact person/department].
